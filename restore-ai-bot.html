India's forest restoration efforts face a critical challenge: inefficient resource allocation. Despite massive government investments, restoration projects consistently fail due to inadequate site assessment and data-driven decision making.

Research indicates that the lack of scientific site selection leads to:

  • High mortality rates of planted saplings
  • Wasted financial resources and human effort
  • Continued environmental degradation
  • Loss of community trust in conservation efforts

The Solution: AI Meets Conservation

Restore AI Bot (@seedsagebot) is a data-driven Telegram bot that transforms how we approach forest restoration. By leveraging artificial intelligence and geospatial data, it provides instant, scientifically-backed plantation viability predictions.

Location Intelligence

Analyzes soil composition, climate patterns, elevation, and historical vegetation data for any given coordinates

Success Prediction

Bilingual Access

Current Focus: Himachal Pradesh

The bot currently specializes in Himachal Pradesh, a state of critical ecological importance in the Indian Himalayas. This region faces unique challenges:

  • Altitude Variations: From 350m to 7,000m elevation requiring species-specific planning
  • Climate Diversity: Subtropical to alpine conditions within the same state
  • Soil Complexity: Varied geological formations affecting plantation success
  • Monsoon Impact: Critical timing considerations for plantation activities

By focusing on this ecologically vital region first, we're building a robust model that can be expanded to other Indian states.
